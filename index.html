X Financial Technologies Launches US-regulated Brokerage for Futures and Options with CFTC License, Expands Global Derivatives Offering
X Financial Technologies, Inc. today announced its expansion into derivatives brokerage services after it received regulatory approval from the NFA to operate as an Independent Introducing Broker in CFTC-regulated derivatives. The Company's new brokerage will enable clients to trade the full range of listed derivatives products – futures, index options, futures options, trade-at-settlement futures, future spreads, and event contracts – on CFTC-registered exchanges.
